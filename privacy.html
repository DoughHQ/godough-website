Privacy Policy

Last updated May 12, 2026

This Privacy Notice for Sunday Company LLC (doing business as Dough) describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and share your personal information when you use our services, including when you visit our website at godough.co or use the Dough mobile application.

Questions or concerns? Reading this privacy notice will help you understand your privacy rights and choices. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services. If you still have any questions or concerns, please contact us at hello@godough.co.

What information do we collect?

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the services, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products, or otherwise contact us.

The personal information we collect may include: email addresses, usernames, and preferences you express through product battles and scans.

We also collect certain information automatically when you use our services, including device information, usage data, and interaction data such as your product battles and scan history.

How do we process your information?

We process your information to provide, improve, and administer our services, communicate with you, and for security and fraud prevention. We process your personal information only when we have a valid legal reason to do so.

Your declared preference data — the choices you make in product battles — is used to build your personal taste profile and to generate aggregated, anonymized insights. Individual preference data is never sold or shared with third parties in identifiable form.

When and with whom do we share your information?

We do not sell your personal information. We may share aggregated, anonymized preference data with food industry partners for research and insights. This data cannot be used to identify you individually.

We may share your information with service providers who assist us in operating our platform, subject to confidentiality agreements.

How do we keep your information safe?

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

What are your privacy rights?

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@godough.co.

Do we make updates to this notice?

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this privacy notice frequently to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
